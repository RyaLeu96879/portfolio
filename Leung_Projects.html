Scratch Project

September 18th-October 9th, 2018, Computer Science Principles

The objective of this project is to create either a game or a story on Scratch.mit.edu. This is the first project for both me and my partner in CSP(Computer Science Principles) which is a course they offered in Dublin High School for people who are interested in coding to take. Me and my partner decide to make a story how a monkey finding its banana when he lost it. The project included us using the code in Scratch putting different block of codes together that Scratch already provided us to make the story happen.Our project included different choice for the player to choose from to make the story for them different and provide different way to reach an end making it funnerfor the player. We needed to document the project to keep in track of what we did in each day of this project, we also needed to follow the criteria in the rubric and using to teamwork to accomplish this project
